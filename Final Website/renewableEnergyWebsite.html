Renewable Energy
What is it? How to Support Quiz
Fossil fuels, and other harmful energy sources similar to it are hurting our environment. This is why I think that our community should strive to make a change to renewable energy. Renewable energy is a safer way to power our lifes and comes in many different forms, like geothermal and solar energy, and would allow us to still keep up our energy enriched lives without the need of harming our envorinment.
Changing to renewable energy would help keep our environment safer and also protect your local community. Climate-altering gasses are increasing yearly and slowly making our world less safe for its inhabitants. For example, fossil fuel burning causes pollutants such as sulphuric acid to combine with water in the air and cause acidic rain which can kill trees, harm animals, and contaminate water supplies.
